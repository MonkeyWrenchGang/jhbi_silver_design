🚨
Fraud Detection
Real-time scoring at ingest
Velocity counts, amount deviation from party baseline, cross-rail behavioral shift, new counterparty first-contact, off-hours activity — all first-class columns, no JSON parsing.
amount transaction_ts originator_party_id match_rule is_on_us payment_type
📉
Churn Prediction
Party-level engagement scoring
Rail usage recency, frequency, and monetary value (RFM) computed directly from the party ID. Cross-rail activity drop signals disengagement before account closure.
originator_party_id payment_type settlement_amount transaction_ts status
🔍
Anomaly Detection
Unsupervised pattern shift
IBAN country deviation, FX rate outliers, sudden routing number change, new beneficiary + high amount — all detectable via structured columns with a simple isolation forest or Z-score.
iban_country_code fx_rate original_currency_code beneficiary_party_id originator_block_key
💡
Product Propensity
Next-best-action scoring
International wire activity → FX product offer. High Zelle + ACH volume → instant payment upgrade. BillPay usage → autopay enrollment. Rail mix is the signal.
payment_type original_currency_code beneficiary_party_id settlement_amount is_on_us
